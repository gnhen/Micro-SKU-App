# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** March 23, 2026

## 1. Introduction
Welcome to the unofficial Micro SKU Scanner / Micro-SKU-App. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it through our compliance with this policy. This policy describes the types of information we may collect from you or that you may provide when you use the app.

## 2. Information We Do Not Collect
We do not collect, store, or process any personally identifiable information (PII). The app operates locally on your device to scan and interpret barcodes and product data.

## 3. Data Usage
Any data processed by this application (such as SKU numbers or product searches) is used explicitly to query public or standard endpoints for product information. This query data is not associated with personal identities and is not retained on our servers.

## 4. Local Storage
The app may utilize local storage on your device to save your preferences, optional default tabs, and recent history. This data remains on your device and is not transmitted externally.

## 5. Third-Party Services
The app may connect to third-party endpoints, such as GitHub to check for open-source app updates or form providers for feedback submission. Interactions with these services are subject to their respective privacy policies. 

## 6. Children's Privacy
Our application is not intended for children under 13 years of age, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## 7. Changes to Our Privacy Policy
We may update our privacy policy from time to time. If we make material changes to how we treat user information, we will notify you by updating the effective date at the top of this policy.

## 8. Contact Information
If you have any questions or comments about this privacy policy and our privacy practices, please contact us via the in-app feedback form or the project repository.
